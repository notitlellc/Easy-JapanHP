- About trial operation -

　This service is currently in trial operation. In order to develop this service as a business later on, our goal is to have customers actually use it and reflect customer feedback more fully. Therefore, we ask our customers to cooperate with our survey.
　Additionally, all plans are currently available for free, but we are considering making this service a paid service after a certain period of time. Currently, we do not have a concrete plan for the period, but we plan to make an announcement as soon as it is decided. If you apply during the trial period, there will be no charge in principle. However, please note that if you apply for continuation or reapply after the monitoring period ends, the regular fee will be charged. (*In that case, the staff will confirm.)
